Help

Enter an exit name to go in that direction.

  • go way to go to another place (or just g, or just the way if it doesn't match the name of another command)
  • look to review the description of your current location (or just l)
  • who to see who else is here (or just w)
  • say comments (or just prefix with a single quote)
  • say (with no comment) to enter chat mode, where all commands are chats. use / to get back out or as a prefix to issue commands from chat mode.
  • nick name or name name to choose your own name.
  • greet to tell others your name.
  • i emote to do your own thing. If your first word is an irregular verb, you can specify the conjugation like i do/does a jig, or conjugate the entire predicate with i do a jig / does a jig
